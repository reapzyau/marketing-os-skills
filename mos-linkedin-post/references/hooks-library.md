# Hooks Library

Categorized hook formulas sourced from Matt Gray's 280 hooks + Justin Welsh's Content OS patterns. Each category includes formula patterns and fill-in examples.

The hook is 80% of your post's success (Gray). Write the hook first. Write 3 options. Pick the best one.

---

## Hook Rules

1. **LinkedIn:** Hook must be under 125 characters (before the "see more" fold)
2. **Purpose of line 1:** Get them to read line 2. That's it.
3. **Test hooks:** Same content with different hooks can get 14X difference in performance (Welsh proved this)

---

## Category 0: Data Authority (Highest-Performing Pattern)

Lead with original research or analysis. Specific numbers in the hook signal credibility and promise unique insights the reader can't find elsewhere. This pattern consistently outperforms all others for enterprise-targeting thought leadership.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| I analysed [specific number] [things] to [find/understand] [insight]. | I analysed 1.2 million search results to understand how ChatGPT cites content. |
| My analysis of [number] [data points] identified [finding]. | My analysis of 11,022 citations identified specific linguistic characteristics that distinguish cited text. |
| I [studied/tracked/looked at] [number] [things] to see [question]. | I looked at ~35,000 ChatGPT citations to understand the presence of review platforms along the buyer journey. |
| [Number] [data points] reveal [surprising finding]. | 4,000+ keywords and almost 40,000 product grids reveal a massive shift. |
| We tracked [metric] across [number] [prompts/queries/pages]. | We tracked how ChatGPT recommends meal delivery services across 200 prompts. |
| I audited [number] [thing] for [purpose]. Here's what I found. | I audited 50 fitness brand websites for AI search readiness. Here's what I found. |

**Why it works:** Specific numbers ("1.2 million", "11,022", "35,000") are more credible than vague claims. The reader knows they'll get original insights unavailable elsewhere. Pair with a chart or data visualisation image for ~2x engagement vs text-only.

**Best combined with:** An image (chart, screenshot, or data viz). Posts using this hook + image averaged 2x the engagement of text-only equivalents.

## Category 1: Curiosity

Create an information gap the reader must close.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| The biggest mistake I made as a [role]? | The biggest mistake I made as a founder? |
| [Number] things nobody tells you about [topic] | 7 things nobody tells you about SEO |
| I spent [time] on [thing] and here's what worked | I spent 6 months on LinkedIn and here's what worked |
| This is going to sound too simple but... | This is going to sound too simple but... |
| What [number] years of [thing] taught me: | What 5 years of content creation taught me: |
| I was wrong about [topic]. Here's why. | I was wrong about AI content. Here's why. |
| The [topic] advice I wish I got [time] ago | The marketing advice I wish I got 3 years ago |
| Here's what nobody talks about when it comes to [topic] | Here's what nobody talks about when it comes to pricing |

## Category 2: Recognition

Make the reader see themselves immediately.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| If you're [identity doing thing], read this: | If you're a gym owner running Meta ads, read this: |
| There are two types of [people]: | There are two types of business owners: |
| You don't need [common advice]. You need [real thing]. | You don't need more leads. You need better offers. |
| If you are [situation]: Stop. | If you are posting daily with no strategy: Stop. |
| Signs of [quality/trait]: | Signs of a business that's about to scale: |
| [Number] things you notice when [situation] | 5 things you notice when your content starts working |
| You're not [negative]. You're just [reframe]. | You're not lazy. You're just building the wrong thing. |
| Most [people] think [X]. The best ones know [Y]. | Most marketers think reach matters. The best ones know saves do. |

## Category 3: Controversy

Challenge what the audience believes. Must be defensible.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Unpopular opinion: [bold claim] | Unpopular opinion: daily posting is a waste of time |
| [Common advice] is terrible advice. Here's why: | "Post every day" is terrible advice. Here's why: |
| Hot take: [contrarian statement] | Hot take: followers don't matter until 10K |
| Stop [thing everyone does]. Start [better thing]. | Stop chasing virality. Start chasing saves. |
| [X] is dead. Here's what replaced it. | Cold outreach is dead. Here's what replaced it. |
| Everyone says [X]. They're wrong. | Everyone says you need a niche. They're wrong. |
| The unsexy truth about [topic]: | The unsexy truth about building an audience: |
| [Thing] is overrated. [Other thing] is underrated. | Consistency is overrated. Quality is underrated. |

## Category 4: Story

Open a narrative loop that demands resolution.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| [Time] ago, I [relatable situation]. | 2 years ago, I was posting into the void. |
| A lesson that still stings me: | A lesson that still stings me: |
| I almost [quit/failed/gave up] because [reason]. | I almost quit content because nobody engaged. |
| Last [week/month], something happened that changed how I [topic]. | Last month, something happened that changed how I think about AI. |
| Being straight with everyone: | Being straight with everyone: |
| Fair warning — this one's personal. | Fair warning — this one's personal. |
| I was skeptical about [thing] until I saw [evidence]. | I was skeptical about AI writing until I saw the actual output. |
| The day I [pivotal moment], everything changed. | The day I stopped chasing followers, everything changed. |

## Category 5: Enemy-Driven

Name a shared enemy or frustration. Creates tribal identity.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| [Industry/tool/approach] is broken. Here's the fix. | Social media marketing is broken. Here's the fix. |
| Why [common thing] doesn't work anymore: | Why posting and praying doesn't work anymore: |
| [Group] don't want you to know this about [topic]: | Agencies don't want you to know this about SEO: |
| Tired of [pain point]? Here's what actually works: | Tired of content that gets 12 views? Here's what actually works: |
| The [industry] playbook is outdated. Here's the new one. | The fitness marketing playbook is outdated. Here's the new one. |
| Stop paying for [thing] that [doesn't work]. | Stop paying for courses that don't give you systems. |

| Formula | Example |

| [N] habits that [changed/transformed] my [area]: | 5 habits that transformed my mornings: |

| [N] rules I follow for [outcome]: | 3 rules I follow for every piece of content: |
| [N] of the best [things] people should know about: | 10 of the best free AI tools people should know about: |
| You can [do X] with just [N] things: | You can build a content system with just 3 tools: |
| [N] signs [positive/negative situation]: | 6 signs your content strategy is working: |
| [N] dead simple steps to [outcome]: | 5 dead simple steps to writing LinkedIn posts that get saved: |

## Category 8: Question

Force the reader to answer internally, creating engagement.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| What would you do if [scenario]? | What would you do if your best content got 0 views? |
| Why do most [people] [fail at thing]? | Why do most gym owners fail at content? |
| What's the difference between [X] and [Y]? | What's the difference between posting and publishing? |
| How do you [thing]? (Here's how I do it:) | How do you decide what to post? Here's how I do it: |
| When was the last time you [examined thing]? | When was the last time you audited your content? |
| What if [contrarian possibility]? | What if you only posted 3 times a week? |
